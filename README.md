# Pluto Time Lite
A (free) cross-platform app for determining when the next Pluto Time will occur.\
This free version has the following limitations:
- No other planet/moon times are offered besides Pluto
- Only one city's Pluto Time can be tracked at a time
- Static city list is reduced from 160000 to 500
- No notifications will be sent reminding you of Pluto Times 5 or 10 minutes before they occur
